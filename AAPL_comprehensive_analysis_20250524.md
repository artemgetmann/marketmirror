## 2. Recent News

**Regulatory Issues:**

- **EU Compliance:** In January 2024, Apple announced significant changes to its App Store and services to comply with European Union competition laws. Effective March 7, 2024, these changes allow iOS users in the EU to access alternative app stores and payment methods, and offer a menu in Safari for downloading alternative browsers like Chrome or Firefox. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc.?utm_source=openai))

**Product Launches:**

- **Apple Intelligence:** In June 2024, Apple introduced "Apple Intelligence," incorporating on-device artificial intelligence capabilities into its ecosystem. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc.?utm_source=openai))

- **Pixelmator Acquisition:** On November 1, 2024, Apple acquired Pixelmator, a company renowned for its image editing applications for iPhone and Mac. Pixelmator's apps have been highlighted in Apple's product launches, including being named Mac App of the Year in 2018 for innovative use of machine learning and AI. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc.?utm_source=openai))

**Legal Settlements:**

- **Siri Privacy Lawsuit:** On December 31, 2024, Apple agreed to a $95 million settlement in a lawsuit alleging that its Siri assistant unlawfully recorded private conversations through unintentional activations and shared them with third parties, including advertisers. Affected users may claim up to $20 per device. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc.?utm_source=openai))
