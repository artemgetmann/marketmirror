## 2. Recent News

**Profit Decline and Leadership Focus**

In April 2025, Tesla reported a significant 71% drop in profits and a 9% decline in revenue for the first quarter. CEO Elon Musk announced he would reduce his involvement in governmental activities to refocus on Tesla's operations. This decision came amid declining vehicle sales and increased competition in the electric vehicle market. ([apnews.com](https://apnews.com/article/2d0b5607c26c56761a436af855199513?utm_source=openai))

**Political Controversies Impacting Sales**

Musk's political affiliations have led to public backlash, including protests and vandalism at Tesla showrooms. These controversies have been correlated with decreased sales, particularly in Europe, and a substantial decline in Tesla's stock price. ([ft.com](https://www.ft.com/content/4d10da88-5a0b-49b5-9b29-77286f1985ec?utm_source=openai))

**Product Developments**

Tesla reaffirmed plans to launch a lower-cost Model Y and introduce a driverless robotaxi service in Austin by June 2025. However, skepticism remains regarding the readiness of Tesla's self-driving technology, which is under federal investigation. ([apnews.com](https://apnews.com/article/2d0b5607c26c56761a436af855199513?utm_source=openai))
