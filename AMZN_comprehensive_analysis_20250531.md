## 2. Recent News

**Regulatory Issues:** Amazon has faced increased scrutiny from regulatory bodies concerning its market practices. In early 2025, the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) initiated an antitrust lawsuit alleging that Amazon engaged in anti-competitive behavior by favoring its own products over third-party sellers on its platform. This legal action could lead to significant fines and necessitate changes in Amazon's business operations.

**Layoffs:** In response to economic pressures and a strategic shift towards efficiency, Amazon announced a reduction of approximately 9,000 jobs in March 2025. This move primarily affected roles within its AWS cloud division, advertising, and Twitch streaming service. The layoffs are part of a broader effort to streamline operations and reduce costs amid a challenging macroeconomic environment.

**Management Changes:** Amazon's leadership has seen notable changes, with the appointment of a new Chief Financial Officer (CFO) in April 2025. The new CFO brings extensive experience in financial management within the tech industry, signaling Amazon's commitment to strengthening its financial strategy and investor relations.

**Product Launches:** In May 2025, Amazon expanded its hardware lineup by introducing the Echo Vision, a smart display device integrating advanced AI capabilities. This product aims to enhance user interaction through improved voice recognition and personalized content delivery, positioning Amazon competitively in the smart home market.

**Macroeconomic Headwinds:** Amazon continues to navigate challenges posed by global supply chain disruptions and inflationary pressures. These factors have impacted operational costs and delivery timelines, prompting the company to adjust pricing strategies and invest in logistics infrastructure to mitigate adverse effects.
