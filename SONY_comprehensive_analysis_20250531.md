## 2. Recent News

**Financial Services Spin-Off**

On May 29, 2025, Sony announced plans to spin off its financial services arm, Sony Financial Group, aiming to enhance the unit's independent fundraising capabilities while maintaining the Sony brand and ongoing collaboration with the parent group. The spin-off is scheduled for September 29, marking Japan's first partial spin-off with a direct listing in over two decades. Sony intends to distribute over 80% of its shares in Sony Financial Group to shareholders via dividends in kind. The financial group also plans to repurchase shares worth approximately 100 billion yen by March 2027.
