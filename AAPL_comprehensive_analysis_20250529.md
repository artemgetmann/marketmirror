## 2. Recent News

**Regulatory Issues:**

- **European Union Compliance:** In January 2024, Apple announced significant changes to its App Store and services to comply with EU competition laws. Effective March 7, 2024, these changes allow iOS users in the 27-nation bloc to utilize alternative app stores and payment methods within apps. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Inc.?utm_source=openai))

- **Digital Markets Act Violation:** On June 24, 2024, the European Commission accused Apple of violating the Digital Markets Act by restricting app developers from directing consumers to alternative channels. - **Conflict Minerals Allegations:** In April 2024, the Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) alleged that Apple sourced minerals from conflict areas in eastern Congo. Despite Apple's mitigation efforts, discrepancies in supplier reporting were identified. In December 2024, the DRC filed a lawsuit against Apple's European subsidiaries. - **Gender Discrimination Lawsuit:** On June 13, 2024, a lawsuit was filed on behalf of 12,000 current and former female employees in California, alleging gender pay bias and sexual harassment at Apple. ([en.wikipedia.org](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AppleToo?utm_source=openai))

**Product Launches:**
